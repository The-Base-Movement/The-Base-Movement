If the user invokes this skill without any other guidance, ask them what they
want to build or design, ask some questions, and act as an expert designer who
outputs HTML artifacts _or_ production code, depending on the need.

Critical brand non-negotiables:
- Three brand colors used as a SYSTEM, never alone: red `#CE1126`, gold `#DAA520`, green `#006B3F`. Black `#000` for ink.
- Sentence case everywhere. No `UPPERCASE`, no `tracking-widest`, no `font-black`. (See typography_modernization protocol.)
- Public Sans for display/meta, Work Sans for body.
- The 3-stripe **BrandLine** (`128 × 6 px`, red→gold→green) appears under every major heading.
- Cards use small radii (4 px) and subtle shadows — never bluish-purple gradients.
- Surfaces are a slightly green-warm off-white (`#f6fbf4`), never pure white.
- Lucide outline icons + Material Symbols. No emoji in product UI.
