How to register a new patriot in 90 seconds.

Use this at markets, taxi ranks, churches, and community events. Keep one in your pocket at all times.

Five steps. Ninety seconds.

1
Start with a questionAsk: "Are you registered to vote?" It opens the door. Never lead with the party.
2
Name one local promiseMention the industrial park for your region. Specifics convert. Slogans don't.
3
Open the app or the formUse your canvasser tablet. The Ghana Card OCR fills name and number automatically.
4
MoMo verify in 30 secThey approve the prompt on their phone. Done. They're in.

Hand them this flyerTell them the next chapter meeting date. Give them the WhatsApp number.
